Eris Emerald Changelog

Go to bit.ly/ErisDoc for documentation and more info about the hack

v0.3.0 - (14 July 2022)
=======================

Mass Outbreaks:
- Unlocked after beating the 3rd Gym
- Species/Location/Levels based on number of badges
- Pulls from the Encounter tables of the route
  - 8% chance of a rare outbreak of Pokemon not normally obtainable
    in the wild
- Increased shiny odds (25 extra rolls, same as in Legends Arceus)
- Random egg move and 50% chance of Hidden Ability
- More than one outbreak per day
  - First outbreak guaranteed, succeeding outbreaks have 5% chance of
    appearing after each battle
  - Avoids repeats of the same route/being on water
- Outbreak species and location shows up in Map

Post Game:
- Added special trainer encounter at northern end of Battle Frontier
- Added ability to get Mystery Gift ticket items
- Added more legendary encounters using Slates at Faraway Island
  - Slates can be purchased using Mystery Shards
- Added Heatran encounter in Scorched Slab
- Added Regigigas encounter in Regice's cave (requires all 3 Regis in
  party)
- Rival battle based on ORAS encounter

Other additions:
- Type-based palette shifting implemented for all Pokemon that can be
  encountered in the base game without randomizing species
- Choose a Randomizer Seed (0000 uses the Trainer ID for backwards
  compatibility)
- More species randomizer options, including a species filter
- Level Cap option (choose between Rare Candy mode, Normal, or Hard)
- Type Charm item which increases odds of same-type encounters when held
  by Pokemon in party

Improvements:
- Regional dex increased to 227 Pokemon
- Shiny type-randomized Pokemon are more distinguishable
- Pokemon that change type upon evolution (like Eeveelutions) will now
  have different types when Type Randomization is on
  - Might cause these Pokemon from previous saves to change type
- Some challenge mode teams have been improved
- Other minor improvements

Quality of life:
- Check randomizer options from any PC under Player's PC
- Rare candies can be purchased at marts
- Shiny odds increased to the modern 1/4096
- All Pokemon in Regional Dex are encounterable
  - Lunatone replaces Solrock encounters at nighttime
  - All other encounters through Mass Outbreaks
- Move relearner won't ask for more Heart Scales after 10 given
  - Heart Scales can be purchased from man to the right of Battle Tent in
    Fallarbor town
- Low health sound effect now only beeps a few times before fading away
- Press B in a wild battle to run away (now shows B icon next to RUN)
- Hours of time-based evolutions changed (4:00-18:00 for daytime,
  nighttime otherwise)
- Money limit increased to 9999999
- Breeding while holding Everstone guarantees nature inheritance

Bug fixes:
- Abilities that influence type encounters now work properly with type
randomization
- Other minor bug fixes
